Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yooga. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website yooga.com or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Sign up for classes
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us through forms or email

Information may include your name, email address, phone number, and health preferences related to yoga practice.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide yoga class bookings and updates
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Send wellness newsletters and promotional content (if opted in)
  • Improve our services and website experience

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers (such as email marketing tools) only for business purposes related to Yooga.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies if you prefer.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct or update your information
  • Delete your data from our records

To make such a request, please contact us at info@yooga.com.

7. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect your information. However, please understand that no online system is 100% secure.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with the updated date.
